📖 What is an Agentic AI Application?

An Agentic AI is an AI system that can autonomously take actions to achieve a goal. Unlike a regular chatbot that just answers questions, an Agentic AI can plan, use tools (like searching the web, reading files, calling APIs), observe results, and decide what to do next — all by itself.

Think of it like this: A regular AI is like a consultant who gives advice. An Agentic AI is like an employee who actually does the work for you.

🗣️ Regular LLM (ChatGPT-style)

User asks → AI thinks → AI responds

Example: "How do I book a flight?"
AI explains the steps but doesn't book it.

🤖 Agentic AI

User asks → AI plans → AI uses tools → AI checks results → AI responds with completed task

Example: "Book me a flight to NYC"
AI searches flights, compares prices, books the best one, sends confirmation.

🔑 Key Concepts in Agentic AI

🎯 Goal-Oriented

Unlike regular chatbots, agents have a clear goal and work towards completing it, not just answering questions.

🔧 Tool Use

Agents can use external tools (APIs, databases, web search) to gather information and take actions.

🔄 Iterative Loop

Agents work in loops: Plan → Act → Observe → Re-plan. They keep going until the goal is achieved.

🧠 Reasoning

After each action, the agent reasons about the result and decides what to do next autonomously.

📝 Memory

Agents maintain memory of past actions and results to inform future decisions.

⚠️ Autonomy Risk

Because agents act autonomously, they need guardrails to prevent unintended or harmful actions.
