What Happened:

3️⃣ AI #2 trusted AI #1 → approved

4️⃣ AI #3 trusted AI #2 → calculated big payout

5️⃣ AI #4 trusted AI #3 → sent real money

💀 Result: $2.25 MILLION STOLEN!

🎯 This is "Cascading Failure"

ONE lie at the start → EVERY AI gets fooled → WHOLE SYSTEM fails!

🛡️
How to Stop This?

Rule: Don't just trust → VERIFY!

❌ Bad System

AI #1 → AI #2 → AI #3 → AI #4

"I trust you"
"I trust you"
"I trust you"

One lie breaks everything!

✅ Good System

AI #1 → CHECK → AI #2 → CHECK → AI #3

"Let me verify this"
"Is this amount normal?"
"Get human approval"

Lie gets caught early!

Simple Fixes:

✅ Verify - Don't just trust, check the facts

✅ Limits - "$2.5M is too high! Stop and check!"

✅ Human Review - Big amounts need human approval

✅ Double Check - Call the hospital to confirm

📝 Remember This:

"A chain is only as strong as its weakest link.

Attack the weakest link → the whole chain breaks."
